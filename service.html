Our Services

Designed to meet the unique needs of each individual, ensuring a safe, healthy, and positive pregnancy experience.

Virtual Consultations

Connect with maternal health experts for personalized advice and support.

Maternity Boarding Service

Offering boarding facilities for expectant mothers in remote areas, ensuring access to medical care one to two months before delivery and several days post-delivery monitoring.

Mobile Clinic Initiative

Extending Maternal Healthcare Reach Through Mobile Clinics Across Kenya's Diverse Communities.

Postpartum Support

Our postpartum support ensures smooth motherhood transition with breastfeeding help and emotional well-being checks.

Prenatal Care

Comprehensive prenatal and postpartum care with check-ups, ultrasounds, classes, and lactation and emotional support.

Community

Join our community for baby names, due date discussions, local groups, and explore popular topics like pregnancy and babies.
